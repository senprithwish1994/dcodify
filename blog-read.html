How machine Learning is Transforming Content Marketing!

These days, "Artificial Intelligence" is becoming the revolutionized innovation in almost every industry (like self-driving cars, spam detecting, our assistants like Siri, Alexa, and much more). As days are passing on, AI will become an even more integral part of our world. Digital marketing is one of the new potential use cases for AI.

Machine learning is a subset of artificial intelligence where computer algorithms are programmed to learn on their own from new and large sets of data ("Big Data") to perform specific actions. These self-driving systems adapt and change their actions based on historical data and patterns, and the more crucial information they gather, the more accurate they become. One of the sectors, where machine learning has made our life easier, is content marketing.

Content marketing helps you to improve conversions because it allows you to connect with and educate your leads and customers. Not only you are working to build trust and relationships, but you are also encouraged conversions by giving consumers the information they need to make an educated purchasing decision.

It has many benefits like increasing your brand awareness, visibility of your brand, developing lasting relationships with your audience, and much more. It is challenging to keep our content updated and valuable for our audience. It takes money and time to do this, but thanks to ML that has evolved in today's digital age.

Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) are fascinating in that they benefit every facet of content marketing. Thanks to ML technology, the process of creating content marketing strategies has become more comfortable.

With the help of Big data, Ml can analyse data and create insights. It can also build a personalized strategy that will help the content marketers to have a better chance of creating compelling, valuable, and customized content that’s easy to find on the SERPs, which will gain engagements. Machine Learning can perform beyond simple content creation; it will help marketers to make better use of the material to have more impact. For example, Spy Fu is a tool that analyses both competitor strategies and user behaviour to engage with the potential audience.

Machine learning-powered tools can find the best content out on the web, including thought leadership pieces, brand-relevant user-generated content, and other content types. The technology can then determine how engaging the content is within real-time, and how it can be pushed out across channels.

Here are some ways by which Machine Learning can transform Content Marketing:

  1. Improving productivity - Mundane tasks like keyword analysis (collecting the highest search volume keywords with the lowest keyword difficulty) can be done quickly by intelligent automation. Through this, we will be able to spend more time and energy on content writing. We can optimize the process of creating content by the right data-powered content management system.
  2. Targeted and Personalized Content - Based on user interaction with content, ML algorithms can foresee what kinds of subjects the audience is interested in. We can customize our data using this technology to get the right audience. This will help in providing specific solutions to the customer.
  3. Finding the Right Content - To write in-depth and valuable content, finding the search intent of the audience is the crucial element. AI gathers relevant data like trending topics and popular search queries on the search engines and predicts the future of your audience.
  4. Data Tracking- Digital marketing success depends on gathering and analysing the results and figuring out what works and what doesn't. The use of data tracking entirely depends on predicting the future based on historical behaviour – which is what ML computers are trained to do, based on real-time data.
  5. Reduced cost - Machine learning can deliver instructions to create a data-driven strategy and help ideate good content. As computers can do repetitive tasks quickly and efficiently, that frees you up in terms of both time and money spent.
  6. Content automation -Machine learning algorithms can understand the English language and provide guidelines to improve the content, like detecting the passive voice or excluding clichés. They can also accomplish more advanced tasks.
  7. AI for Video Content - With this feature, machines can recognize the content in images and videos. Some companies have already started experimenting with AI and video, and the results are quite exciting.


Machines are incredible for collecting information and helping writers ideate, create, share, and analyse more compelling content for their targeted audience.
